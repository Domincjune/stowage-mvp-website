Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

ShipIQ.ai ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and business data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our B2B SaaS platform for optimizing cargo operations.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, company name, job title, email address, contact number.
  • Operational Data: Cargo details, vessel schedules, stowage plan information, and optimization parameters provided by you or generated by our AI models.
  • Maritime-Specific Data:
    • Vessel specifications (IMO numbers, dimensions, stability data)
    • Cargo details (hazardous material classifications, temperature requirements)
    • Port and terminal operational constraints
    • Voyage-specific data (ETA/ETD, bunker requirements)
  • Usage Data: Login activity, feature usage, device/browser type, IP address, and cookies.
  • Support & Communication Records: Any inquiries, feedback, or support requests you send us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our AI-driven stowage optimization services.
  • Customize your experience on our platform.
  • Communicate important updates, product enhancements, or maintenance notices.
  • Ensure system security, detect fraud, and comply with applicable laws.
  • Generate anonymized analytics for performance benchmarking.
  • Ensure compliance with SOLAS, IMDG, and IMO regulations.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share data only with:

  • Subprocessors: Cloud providers (AWS, Azure), maritime data aggregators
  • Legal Compliance: Port state control, flag state authorities, or court orders
  • Business Transactions: Mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales

4. Data Security

We use enterprise-grade security measures including:

  • Encryption: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions (e.g., ship operators vs. shore staff)
  • Audit Logs: Track all data modifications

5. Data Retention

We retain data according to maritime industry standards:

  • Operational voyage data: 7 years (aligned with common maritime documentation requirements)
  • User account data: Until account termination + 2 years
  • Anonymized optimization data: Indefinitely for product improvement

6. International Data Transfers

Given the global nature of shipping operations, data may be transferred to and processed in:

  • Singapore (Primary hosting location)
  • EU/EEA countries (For European clients under GDPR)
  • Other jurisdictions where our clients' vessels operate

We implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for all international transfers.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, correct, or delete personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Restrict processing for sensitive voyage data
  • Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for authentication, analytics, and improving user experience.

9. Data Subject Rights for Seafarers

We recognize special considerations for:

  • Seafarer data processed through client accounts
