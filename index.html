How ShipIQ Works?

From cargo nomination to shipboard confirmation, an AI-powered loop that makes every plan smarter than the last.

1

Cargo & Vessel Input

Operators enter cargo details, vessel specs, and voyage constraints (MOLCO, MOLOO, SG, tank wash status).

2

AI-Generated Draft Plan

ShipIQ instantly produces an optimized stowage suggestion based on tank logic, stability principles, and past patterns.

3

Operator Review & Adjust

Ops teams tweak or validate the plan through an intuitive interface, keeping human expertise in control.

5

Fleet Intelligence Engine

Each completed plan feeds back into ShipIQ’s models, improving accuracy and adaptability fleet-wide.

4

Export & Ship-Shore Exchange

Finalized plan is exported in Excel/PDF for vessel use, ensuring seamless communication with existing workflows.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 9, 2025

At ShipIQ.ai, we respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our AI-driven cargo optimization platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, company name, job title, email, phone number.
  • Operational Data: Cargo details, vessel schedules, stowage plans, and AI optimization outputs.
  • Maritime-Specific Data: Vessel specs, hazardous cargo data, port restrictions, voyage data.
  • Usage Data: Login activity, feature usage, device/browser type, IP address, cookies.
  • Support Records: Queries, feedback, or communication with our team.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our AI optimization services.
  • Enhance user experience and customization.
  • Send updates, product changes, and service notices.
  • Ensure compliance with maritime regulations (SOLAS, IMO, IMDG).
  • Generate anonymized analytics for product improvement.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. We only share with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud and data partners (e.g., AWS, Azure).
  • Authorities: When legally required (port state, flag state).
  • Business Transactions: In mergers, acquisitions, or restructures.

4. Security & Retention

  • Security: AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3, role-based access, and audit logs.
  • Retention: Voyage data kept 7 years, account data until closure + 2 years, anonymized data indefinitely.

5. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in Singapore, EU/EEA, and other jurisdictions. We follow Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure protection.

6. Your Rights
